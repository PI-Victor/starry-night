Starry Night
Tue, Apr 19, 2016

Amanrath

Chapter I - Acquaintances

Bare foot stepping on the muddy soil through the woods. Each step colder than the last, while the rain never ceased pouring over cold soil. The wind was blowing through the trees in the vast solitude of the Boar Forest, biting like a thousand blades that just want to rip the skin off of meat. Distant fog was hiding the peak of the pines, like a veil of smoke engulfing dead nature in a fore fire.
